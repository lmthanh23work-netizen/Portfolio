(About Me)

framing

emotion,

shaping

memory.

My exploration of a film as an attention exercise focuses on the use of light, texture, and camera movement to construct fleeting human moments that transcend language. In addition to my meticulous technical knowledge in areas like lighting design, lens selection, camera choreography, and colour narrative, I also possess an adventurous curiosity that welcomes set surprises.

I'm hoping to refine the abilities that turn intuition into grammar, broaden my visual language, and put formal ideas to the test alongside supportive partners by enrolling in film school. I am committed to movie that delves into themes of empathy, memory, and identity, and my hunger for risk-taking has developed into demanding work habits. I'm ready to learn more, get advice from more experienced people, and contribute my unique viewpoint to a creative group that values technique more than originality.
